Awards
  • For the year 2013-14, NU was awarded the First Prize for Excellent Performance in Promotion of Official Language by the Ministry of Commerce and Industry.
  • On 14.09.2014, NU was awarded the Third Prize of Indira Gandhi Official Language for its Excellent Performance for the year 2012-13 by the Hon'ble President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee.
  • Our company got the distinction of being the 3rd best Public Sector Enterprise for use of Hindi in its official work and was awarded the Indira Gandhi Rajbhasha Puraskar, by the Home Ministry, Government of India for the year 2012-13.
  • During the year, EEPC selected NU for EEPC India (NR) Award for 2011-12 under the category "Star Performers in Product Groups for 2011-12 (Shield), FerroAlloys (Large Enterprise)".
